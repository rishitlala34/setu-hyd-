Step-by-Step Process
1
Step 1
Login
Takes under a minute

Enter your 12-digit Aadhaar number + captcha. Enter the OTP sent to your mobile.

💡 OTP expires in 10 mins. Not received? Click "Resend OTP" after 60 seconds.
Open myAadhaar
2
Step 2
Go to Address Update
Once logged in
1
Click "Update Demographics Data" on the dashboard.
↓
2

Your proof, your name
1

2
Form opens → go to Step 3.
Family member's proof
1
Enter Head of Family's Aadhaar number and your relationship.
2

3
Pay ₹75. HoF ને SMS — 30 દિવસ માં approve કરવું પડશે.
3
Step 3
Fill the Form
English only · PDF or JPEG · under 2 MB
1
Fill: House No., Street, Locality, Town, District, State, PIN Code — English only.
↓
2
Pick your document type, then upload. PDF or JPEG, under 2 MB.
↓
!
PIN code must match your document exactly — most common reason for rejection.

✅ Accepted documents:

  • Passport
  • Voter ID (EPIC)
  • Driving Licence
  • Bank / Post Office Passbook
  • Electricity Bill (≤3 months)
  • Water / Gas Bill (≤3 months)
  • Ration Card
  • Registered Rent Agreement
  • Property Tax Receipt
  • Registered Sale / Gift Deed
⚠️ Check before submitting — common reasons for rejection
1
PIN code mismatch — must be identical to your document.
2
Name mismatch — document name must match Aadhaar. Use HoF if it's a family member's document.
3
Blurry scan — must be a clear colour scan. Screenshots are rejected.
📝 Address typed must exactly match your document — even "St." vs "Street" matters.
✅ Already logged in from Step 1. Continue on the same tab — do not open a new one.
4
Step 4
Pay
On the UIDAI portal
Address Update Fee
Non-refundable
₹75

Pay via UPI, Net Banking, or Card. Only on the UIDAI portal.

🔢 You'll get a 14-digit SRN after payment. Save it — needed to track in Step 5.
🧾 Error after paying? Note your transaction ID and call 1947.
Fee Details (UIDAI)

Step 5
Save Acknowledgement
Save your URN — you'll need it to track the update

Download or screenshot the acknowledgement slip. It contains your SRN and is your proof of submission.

A confirmation SMS will also be sent to your mobile.

⏳ Processing takes 15–90 days. Use SRN to track.
📲 Once approved, download your updated e-Aadhaar from myaadhaar.uidai.gov.in.
